Sandra&Sofia are mother&daughter yoga instructors. They hope to meet you through their 'YogaVentures' soon

and welcome your contact.

They'd love you to know more about their..

×
Sandra's classes are a model of safe yoga & wellbeing. Through guidance & invitation, individuals learn, enjoy and succeed within their own limitations.
All classes commence with a grounding & long awakening warm up, preparing the joints & the body to work deeper as the lesson progresses.
Often guided with a theme, classes progress with positive affirmations, guided meditations, beautiful visualisations & empowering mantras.
Sessions culminate with soothing, transporting sound baths.
Sessions are a journey & create a full mind, body & spirit work out. Her work is full of positivity & connects you to the beauty of the world & life within & around us.
×
Sandra...
Studying PE at University in Buenos Aires and with 35 years of experience in safe and beneficial body movement, Sandra began her Yoga training with Oscar Bonifacio at home in Argentina & began teaching in the UK 20 years ago, completing CPD in Yoga alongside her practice.
She completed a 200 hour course in Hi Ki yoga, is trained in freestyle fitness Yoga, and 'Buff Bones'. She has also been a swimming, step, aqua & land aerobics instructor.
She still teaches one aqua-aerobics class a week with some of her regular students having attended for over 19 years!

Sofia...

Sofia's background is in dance & she grew up surrounded by Yoga, experiencing and learning from her mother.
Sofia, now qualified, helps with classes, their participation at events, workshops, retreats and their successful 'Yoga holidays'.
In 2016 Sofia travelled to Rishiskesh India, said to be the home of Yoga and completed a 200 hour YTT as a foundation for her own Yoga teaching.
In February 2018 she attended the 'Womb Yoga' course by Uma Dinsmore Tuli & Sivani Matta.
-A course on Yoga's use in women's wellness therapy.

Together..

Being multi-lingual helps both Sandra and Sofia have outstanding communication skills.
They continually attend CPD courses & immersion workshops for teachers with some of their inspirations
...Nisha Srivastava, Uma Dinsmore Tuli, Tanja Mickwitz, Shiva Rea & others, to constantly widen their wisdom, experience & practice.
Both are also trained in Indian Head & Ayurvedic massage.
×
Sandra's passion is the sharing of love & care.
Her unbounded energy, soothing voice & words from the heart cannot be forgotten. Her south-american heritage, travel and life experiences influence the unique, dynamic style of her yoga.
She invites & integrates creative expression & movement into her work, connecting people to their inner rhythm & fluidity. Her motherly nature welcomes everybody to explore their body & tune into it's message.
Her yoga forms & adapts to benefit all walks of life. Underpinning all her work is a deep desire to help people reach a more happy & balanced state of being.

Sofia's 'YogaVentures' include creating, organising & facilitating workshops, retreats & extremely popular, innovative, 'yoga, meditation & well-being holidays' around the UK & overseas.
She is passionate about inviting different teachers & healers to share their techniques, knowledge and experience with you.
In addition to 'YogaVentures', Sofia works at UK festivals, dancing (normally barefoot) & connecting with people everywhere she goes, often accompanied by her free sensory interactive stall..
..a space for all to come & explore sound, vision, colour & texture, through her sharing of 'stuff'...instruments, fancy dress, tribal paints & more.
She is particularly in love with the element of water.

Together they invite you to join them at a YogaVentures offering, a safe space for anybody that wishes to join in.
They also share their work at Yoga festivals & shows, such as 'World Yoga Festival' & the 'Om Yoga shows'.
Each of us offer such a different perspective on life-bringing people together is something they cherish & feel very grateful to be able to do!
